Collection of R scripts developed for the research project *New approaches to the study of the evolution of new genes and novel gene functions in plants* (acronym: **PlantEvolGen**). The project was supported by a “Proyectos I+D Generación de Conocimiento” grant from the Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation (grant code: PID2020-113277GB-I00). Concretely, we studied the prevalence of plastid DNA in plant nuclear genomes, characterizing the complete set of molecular mechanisms and evolutionary forces that contribute to its copy and relocation from the plastids to the nucleus, ultimately assessing its impact on the evolution of nuclear genome architecture and function.
